Our Committees

Social, Humanitarian and Cultural Committee (SOCHUM)

The Social, Humanitarian and Cultural Committee addresses human rights, social development, minorities, refugees, and cultural preservation.

House of Commons (HOC)

The House of Commons debates and passes laws, scrutinizes government policy, and represents citizens in parliamentary democracy.

Disarmament and International Security Committee (DISEC)

DISEC focuses on disarmament, non-proliferation, and maintaining international peace and security.

The Group of Twenty (G20)

The G20 is a forum for major global economies to discuss trade, development, and climate challenges.

Joint Crisis Committee (JCC)

JCC offers a fast-paced crisis simulation where multiple blocs interact dynamically and affect each other's timelines.

United Nations Security Council (UNSC)

The UNSC maintains international peace and security through binding resolutions and crisis negotiations.
