Common Questions

Frequently asked questions.

Who is SPAMUN for?

+
-

A conference with a variety of committees, SPAMUN is suitable for any delegate! We offer general assemblies to those who are just starting out, regional councils to our more intermediate delegates, and an advanced committee to the delegates who want to challenge themselves!

How is SPAMUN different?

+
-

Because of its smaller size, SPAMUN is more focused on individual delegates. Our committees all have less than 30 delegates each, and as such, we are able to focus on helping you with a better debating experience!

It's my first time participating in model UN. How should I prepare for this conference?

+
-

First off, congrats on signing up! That's a fantastic step in getting started on your model UN journey! Model UN is a fantastic way for you to gain a global perspective, and find your voice. To get started, you can look at the information section of our website. There, you will find several guides that pertain to conference rules of procedure, details about model UN, and helpful information for research!
